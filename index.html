Rutgers First Annual Phield Day!

*************************************************************************************************
Come out to our first annual Rutgers Phield Day to support the United Services Organization (USO) and compete in different activites including Tug-of-War, Three-Legged Race, Wheelbarrow Race, Obstacle Course, Egg Toss and much more. Top 4 teams will play in the playoffs.

Rutgers Phield Day will take place at the Buccleuch Park Pavilion on April 17th starting at 12:30PM. T-shirts will be sold and prizes will be given out to the winning team, best team spirit, and the team that raised the most money! We will provide food and drinks throughout the event. So come out, bring your friends, and help us raise money for the USO and the men and women who fight each day for the freedoms of our country.
*************************************************************************************************

Who can attend?

All Rutgers Students can participate in the event. You must present a valid Rutgers Student ID at the event to enter.

How can I register?

Click on the sign up button at the top of this page. You can either join a team or start a new fundraiser to create a new team. Remember, your minimum goal is $30 to participate in the events. You can also donate at the event as well.

How much does it cost?

There is a minimum enterence fee of $30 per team. You can raise more money as well.

What food will you provide?

We will provide you snacks and drinks throughout the event, as well as other food.

How big should teams be?

Each team must consist of 6 members.

What should I bring?

All members participating must bring a valid Rutgers Student ID for admission.

What are the prizes?

Prizes will be announced. Prizes will be given out to winning team, most team spirit, and the team that raised the most money.

What is the schedule?

Sign in begins at 12:30PM. Events start at 1PM. After all teams complete each event, playoff teams will be annouced.

What if it rains/bad weather?

The event will be canceled for bad weather. All donations will go to USO. Please check our website for weather updates.

Other questions?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding Rutgers Phield Day contact us at contact@ruphieldday.me. We look forward to hearing from you and can't wait to see you!
