SIGN UP!

Rutgers First Annual Phield Day!

Come out to support USO and compete in different activites including Tug-of-War, Three- Legged Race, Wheelbarrow Race, Obstacle Course, Egg Toss and much more. Top 4 teams will play in the playoffs.

T-shirts will be given out to teams that make it to the playoff!

Who can attend?

All Rutgers Students can participate in the event. You must present a valid Rutgers Student ID at the event to enter.

How can I register?

Click on the sign up button at the top of this page.

How much does it cost?

There is a minimum enterence fee of $30 per team. You can raise more money as well.

What food will you provide?

We will provide you snacks and drinks throughout the event, as well as other food.

How big should teams be?

Each team must consist of 6 members.

What should I bring?

All members participating must bring a valid Rutgers Student ID for admission.

What are the prizes?

Prizes will be announced. Prizes will be given out to winning team, most team spirit, and the team that raised the most money.

What is the schedule?

The events start at 12PM. After all teams complete each event, playoff teams will be annouced.

Other questions?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding Rutgers Phield Day contact us at contact@ruphieldday.me. We look forward to hearing from you and can't wait to see you!
